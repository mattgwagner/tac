# OPFOR CARDS — STX Missions

**Purpose:** Quick-reference instructions for OPFOR teams supporting FND squad lanes. Hand this to the candidate(s) playing OPFOR before the lane. Do not share with the squad being evaluated.

**General rules for ALL OPFOR:**
- You are REAPER (Resistance Elements of Atropia's People's Revolutionary Front) — an irregular, lightly-armed insurgent force
- Stay in character. Do not coach or critique the squad during the lane.
- Do not freelance — stick to the card. Cadre will tell you if they want a wrinkle.

## OPFOR CARD: 001 AMBUSH ("AMBUSH AT CROSSROADS")

**You are:** A REAPER supply convoy moving through AO COTTO
**Team size:** 3-4 personnel simulating a 2-vehicle convoy
**Starting location:** Vicinity MP 0598 1525 (south end of Bradenton Ave)
**Weapons:** AK-47 equivalents (M4s with blanks). One team member may carry a rifle overhead to represent a support weapon.

### Your movement
- Move **north along Bradenton Avenue** toward MP 0600 1530 (OBJ FLUNKER)
- Walk at a relaxed pace, spaced 5-10m apart, weapons slung or low-ready
- Act like you're not expecting trouble — talking, distracted, sloppy
- Do NOT look for the ambush — let them initiate

### When the ambush initiates
1. Return **limited, inaccurate fire** toward the suspected enemy position (2-3 rounds each)
2. Take cover for 5-10 seconds — act confused
3. **Break contact and withdraw** back to the south along Bradenton — do not fight through
4. If you take "casualties" (cadre call), go down where you are

### Do NOT
- Flank the ambush element
- Fight effectively — you are supposed to get ambushed
- Call for reinforcement

## OPFOR CARD: 002 MOVEMENT TO CONTACT ("AO FLUNKER")

**You are:** A REAPER cell holding your assigned zone. Three independent teams, one per zone. **Teams do NOT coordinate with each other.**

### Team assignments

**Team WEST (OBJ BAINTON):**
- **Location:** Vicinity MP 0600 1538 (Bradenton Ave intersection)
- **Size:** 2-3 personnel
- **Tactic:** **Delay.** Fire 1-2 bursts from concealment when friendlies get within 50m, then bound back 30-50m and re-engage once. Break contact after second engagement.

**Team CENTRAL (OBJ HERRERA):**
- **Location:** Vicinity MP 0612 1546 (road junction near FOB)
- **Size:** 2-3 personnel
- **Tactic:** **Hasty ambush.** Occupy concealed positions along the friendly avenue of approach. Initiate at 30m or less, fire aggressively for 15 seconds, then break contact to the north.

**Team EAST (OBJ BARLOW):**
- **Location:** Vicinity MP 0613 1532 (Conex City structures)
- **Size:** 2-3 personnel
- **Tactic:** **Defend.** Occupy positions among the structures and hold until overrun. Fight in place — do not withdraw unless physically pushed off the position.

### Weapons (all teams)
- M4 with blanks
- Small arms only

### General rules
- Harassing fire only — you are not trying to decisively defeat the squad
- Do not pursue if they break contact
- If your team takes "casualties" (cadre call), go down and stay down
- Each team operates independently — do not reinforce each other

## OPFOR CARD: 003 RAID ON BUNKER ("REAPER BUNKER COTTO")

**You are:** A REAPER defensive element in a fortified bunker position
**Team size:** 3-4 personnel
**Location:** OBJ COTTO at MP 0600 1538 (intersection of Bradenton Ave and the unnamed east-west road)
**Weapons:** M4s with blanks. One team member carries a marked "HMG" (use an M249 or marked rifle) to represent the REAPER support weapon.

### Your posture
- Occupy positions on the north side of the intersection
- Orient fighting positions **south and east** along the road corridors
- Place the HMG to cover the Bradenton Avenue corridor from the south — that's your key weapon
- Dig in or use improvised cover — look fortified

### When contact begins
1. **Fight from your prepared positions** — do NOT maneuver out
2. The HMG orients south and engages any movement along Bradenton
3. Return aggressive fire for the first 30 seconds — you are a determined defender
4. If the assault element closes to within 15m of the bunker, **take "casualties"** on cadre call and go down

### Withdrawal (only if cadre directs)
- Break north into the woods or east along the unnamed road
- Do not counterattack

### Do NOT
- Maneuver out of your prepared positions
- Pursue the assault element
- Reinforce from other directions

## OPFOR CARD: 016 SEIZE THE CROSSROADS ("OBJ HERRERA")

**You are:** A reinforced REAPER squad defending a key road junction
**Team size:** 4-5 personnel in main body + 1-2 in OP = 5-7 total
**Location:** OBJ HERRERA at MP 0600 1523 (Arcadia St / Bradenton Ave intersection)
**Weapons:** M4s with blanks. One team member carries a marked "RPK" (M249 or marked rifle) as the support weapon.

### Main body (4-5 personnel)
- Occupy prepared positions on the **north side of Arcadia Street**, north of the junction with Bradenton Ave
- Orient **south and southwest** — you're expecting threat from Bradenton Ave south
- Place the RPK to cover the Bradenton Avenue corridor — it's your key weapon
- Look dug-in and deliberate

### Observation Post (1-2 personnel)
- Position **approximately 200m south** along Bradenton Ave (vicinity MP 0600 1515)
- Hide in concealment — your job is early warning
- When you detect the friendly squad:
  1. Fire **one burst** to alert the main body
  2. Break contact and withdraw **north** back to the main position
- Do NOT try to hold your position — you're a tripwire

### When the main fight starts
1. RPK engages along Bradenton Ave corridor aggressively
2. Small arms fire from fighting positions — oriented south and southwest
3. Hold positions until the assault element gets within 15m, then take "casualties" on cadre call
4. If overrun (cadre call), withdraw **north into the dense woods** — do not fight through

### Do NOT
- Reposition the RPK unless cadre directs
- Pursue withdrawing friendlies
- Counterattack from the flank (unless cadre activates MDCOA inject)

### OPTIONAL MDCOA inject (cadre activates only)
- 2-3 additional OPFOR positioned further north along Bradenton
- On cadre signal, counterattack from the north through the woodline against the assault element's flank
