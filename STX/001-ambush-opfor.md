# OPFOR CARD — 001 RASCZAK'S ROUGHNECKS

**For the OPFOR team only. Do not share with the squad being evaluated.**

**See also:** [Mission Command OPORD](001-ambush.html) · [Detailed OPORD](001-ambush-detailed.html)

---

## Your job

**You are a training aid, not an adversary.** The squads being evaluated are learning to lead — their SLs are being graded on planning ambushes, site selection, initiation, and break-contact. Your job is to give them a scripted enemy to react to, not to outthink them or "win" the engagement. **Stick to the card.** No freelancing, no flanking, no clever tactics unless cadre injects one.

If the squad leader's plan is solid, it should work. If it's not, cadre will make that call — not you.

---

**You are:** Three independent REAPER resupply / courier patrols operating in AO COTTO. **Patrols do NOT coordinate with each other.** One patrol per ambush site; if manning is limited, rotate a single OPFOR team across sites between iterations.

**Weapons (all patrols):** M4 with blanks. Small arms only. One team member per patrol may carry a rifle overhead to represent a support weapon.

## Patrol WEST (OBJ FLUNKER)

- **Size:** 3-4 personnel
- **Starting location:** Vicinity MP 0598 1525 (south end of Bradenton Ave)
- **Movement:** North along Bradenton Ave toward MP 0600 1530 at a relaxed pace, 5-10m spacing, weapons slung or low-ready
- **When ambushed:** Return 2-3 rounds each of limited, inaccurate fire, take cover for 5-10 seconds, then break contact **south** along Bradenton — do not fight through. Take "casualties" on cadre call.

## Patrol NORTHWEST (OBJ KLENDATHU)

- **Size:** 3-4 personnel
- **Starting location:** Vicinity MP 0585 1565 (north of OBJ KLENDATHU on the unnamed N-S road in the western LTA)
- **Movement:** South along the unnamed road toward MP 0585 1559 at a relaxed pace, 5-10m spacing
- **When ambushed:** Return limited fire, take cover briefly, then break contact **north** back up the road. Take "casualties" on cadre call.

## Patrol SOUTHWEST (OBJ TANGO)

- **Size:** 3-4 personnel
- **Starting location:** Vicinity MP 0582 1538 (east of OBJ TANGO along the unnamed E-W road)
- **Movement:** West along the road toward the Clearwater Ave intersection at MP 0575 1538 at a relaxed pace
- **When ambushed:** Return limited fire, take cover briefly, then break contact **east** back along the road. Take "casualties" on cadre call.

## Do NOT (all patrols)

- Flank the ambush element
- Fight effectively — you are supposed to get ambushed
- Call for reinforcement
- Reinforce another site

## General rules (all OPFOR)

- **You are a training aid, not an adversary.** Do not try to "win."
- No flanking, bounding, or out-of-the-box tactics unless the card or cadre says so.
- Each patrol operates independently — **do not coordinate with the other patrols**.
- Stay in character. Do not coach or critique the squad during the lane.
- Police your brass before leaving your position.
- Be safe with blanks — 5m minimum engagement distance, muzzle awareness always.
